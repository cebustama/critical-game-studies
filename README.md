# Critical Game Studies

Research, analysis, and design work at the intersection of critical theory, game studies, and systems design.

## Scope

- Materialist analysis of videogame history and mechanics
- Colonialism, imperialism, and extraction logic in game systems
- Military-entertainment complex and propaganda
- Race, gender, and minority representation
- Orientalism and Cold War ideology
- Alternative and resistant design practices
- Game poems and experimental forms

## Analytical Framework

Five-layer method applicable to any game:

1. Material Archaeology (production, distribution, consumption)
2. Systemic Grammar (verbs, nouns, syntax of mechanics)
3. Ideological Operation (what does the player internalize?)
4. Contradictions and Excess (where does the game exceed its ideology?)
5. Positionality of Analyst (who is analyzing, from where?)
